### Details

The Fraud Service emits `Fraud.Not.Detected` when no fraud is found in the analyzed document. Multiple services consume this event with different filter conditions:

- **Customer Service** — updates the customer record when a driver's license passes fraud checks (`documentType: DRIVERS_LICENSE`), and updates the customer's car policy record when a signup car image passes (`documentType: CAR, fraudType: SIGNUP.CAR`)
- **Claims Service** — marks the claim as validated when a claims car image passes (`documentType: CAR, fraudType: CLAIMS`)
- **Settlement Service** — triggers settlement processing when a claims car image passes (`fraudType: CLAIMS`)
- **Notifications Service** — notifies the customer of the outcome for all fraud check results
