### Details

The Documents Service triggers `Document.Processed` upon completing the processing of a customer document. Two document types are processed:

- **DRIVERS_LICENSE** — analyzed using Amazon Textract to extract identity fields (name, DOB, document number, etc.)
- **CAR** — analyzed using Amazon Bedrock Nova 2 Lite to identify vehicle type, color, and damage level

The Fraud Service consumes this event to analyze the document for potential fraud. The Notifications Service also utilizes this event to update the customer regarding the status of their request.
